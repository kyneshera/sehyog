About Us
A Koshish
born in 2018

Sehyog Ek Koshish was founded in 2018 with a simple belief — that every small effort together creates a massive change. We are a community of passionate volunteers who show up every month to make sure no one in our neighbourhood goes hungry, uneducated, or unsupported.

From distributing monthly ration kits to families below the poverty line, to organising blood donation camps and food drives — our work is powered entirely by people who care.

What We Do
Our Programs
🍱

₹10 Meal Initiative

Nutritious hot meals served at just ₹10 — because no one should sleep hungry due to cost.

🛒

Monthly Rashan Drive

Essential grocery & ration kits distributed every month to BPL families in our area.

📚

Book Distribution

Used & new books given to children who can't afford them — keeping the light of learning alive.

🩸

Blood Donation Camps

Organised regular blood donation drives, connecting donors to those in critical need.

🌳

Tree Plantation

Planting trees in schools, parks & roadsides — building a greener tomorrow, one sapling at a time.

🍲

Food Drives

Large-scale food drives during festivals and emergencies to reach the most vulnerable families.

🐾

Animal Food Drive

Feeding stray animals with regular food drives — because compassion has no species boundary.
